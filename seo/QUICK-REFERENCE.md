## Common Mistakes to Avoid

⚠️ **Phase 1-2: Keyword Research**
- Don't skip manual SERP analysis (this finds quick wins!)
- Don't rely only on difficulty scores (check actual competitors)
- Don't assume high volume = best target (transactional intent > volume)

⚠️ **Phase 3: Content Planning**
- Don't write same content for all keywords (match to intent!)
- Don't forget content gaps (this is your competitive advantage)
- Don't ignore audience personas (write for real people)

⚠️ **Phase 4: Competitive Analysis**
- Don't focus on official competitors (go for weak competitors at positions 3-5!)
- Don't ignore stale content (easy to beat with fresh, updated content)
- Don't forget to extract backlink sources (natural outreach targets)

⚠️ **Phase 5: Long-Tail Keywords**
- Don't skip low-volume questions (10-50 searches/month = GOLD)
- Don't write individual posts (group into series for internal linking)
- Don't write 2000-word posts for every question (800-1200 words often enough)

⚠️ **Content Writing**
- Don't publish without SEO checklist (title, meta description, headers, links)
- Don't write without internal linking (3-5 links per post)
- Don't forget visual elements (screenshots, diagrams, videos)
- Don't skip CTA (always link back to "Try Fox in the Box")
