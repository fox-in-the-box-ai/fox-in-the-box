# Rank Tracking Setup

## Method 1: Manual Weekly Check (Recommended to start)

**Effort:** 30 min/week | **Cost:** $0

### Process
1. Every Monday morning, check your top 10 Tier 1 keywords in Google
2. Record ranking position in `06_SEO-MASTER-TRACKER.csv`
3. Note: You're looking for position, not clicks (position matters for SEO leverage)
4. For keywords not in top 50, record as "Not ranking"

### Spreadsheet Columns
- Keyword
- Current_Position (or "Not ranking")
- Check_Date
- Previous_Position (for trend tracking)

## Method 2: Automated SERPstat Free (Optional)

**Effort:** 15 min setup | **Cost:** Free (5 keywords tracked)
**Benefit:** Daily automated tracking for top 5 keywords

1. Sign up at https://serpstat.com (free tier)
2. Add project: foxinthebox.io
3. Add keywords: Top 5 Tier 1 keywords
4. SERPstat checks daily, export data monthly
