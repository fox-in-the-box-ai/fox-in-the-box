## 🎯 7 Phase Guides

### Phase 1: Seed List & Scope (Week 1, Days 1-2)
**File:** `PHASE-1-SEED-LIST.md`
- **Goal:** Build 80-120 seed keywords
- **Time:** 4-6 hours
- **Key Tasks:** 
  - Intent cluster generation (15 core keywords × 5 variations)
  - Competitor SERP analysis (5 competitors, manual review)
  - Trend-chasing (related keywords)
- **Outputs:** keyword-research-master.csv
- **Dependencies:** None (start here!)

### Phase 2: Volume & Difficulty (Week 1, Days 3-5)
**File:** `PHASE-2-VOLUME-DIFFICULTY.md`
- **Goal:** Filter to 40 high-opportunity keywords
- **Time:** 6-8 hours
- **Key Tasks:**
  - Google Keyword Planner research (all keywords)
  - Ubersuggest difficulty scoring (top 15 keywords)
  - Answer The Public question mining
  - Opportunity scoring matrix
- **Outputs:** keyword-targets-final.csv, keyword-opportunity-matrix.csv
- **Dependencies:** Phase 1 ✅

### Phase 3: Search Intent Mapping (Week 2, Days 1-2)
**File:** `PHASE-3-SEARCH-INTENT.md`
- **Goal:** Map keywords to content types + identify gaps
- **Time:** 4-5 hours
- **Key Tasks:**
  - Intent classification (Navigational/Informational/Transactional/Commercial)
  - Content type mapping (Blog/Guide/Landing/Comparison)
  - Manual SERP gap analysis (top 15 keywords)
  - Audience persona definition
  - Content calendar draft
- **Outputs:** keyword-to-content-mapping.csv, content-gap-analysis.md, audience-personas.md
- **Dependencies:** Phase 2 ✅

### Phase 4: Competitive Landscape (Week 2, Days 3-5)
**File:** `PHASE-4-COMPETITIVE-LANDSCAPE.md`
- **Goal:** Analyze top competitors + identify weak spots
- **Time:** 5-6 hours
- **Key Tasks:**
  - Deep SERP analysis (top 5 results × 10 keywords)
  - Competitor quality assessment (1-5 scale)
  - Backlink source mining (where do they get links?)
  - Quick-win identification (weak competitors to beat)
  - Ranking projections (6-month timeline)
- **Outputs:** competitor-serp-analysis.md, quick-win-targets.md, backlink-opportunities.md
- **Dependencies:** Phase 3 ✅

### Phase 5: Long-Tail Keywords (Week 3, Days 1-2)
**File:** `PHASE-5-LONG-TAIL.md`
- **Goal:** Mine question keywords + build blog series
- **Time:** 3-4 hours
- **Key Tasks:**
  - Answer The Public mining (question variations)
  - Google autocomplete analysis
  - Reddit pain point mining
  - Blog series grouping (3-5 posts per series)
  - Content calendar integration
- **Outputs:** long-tail-question-keywords.csv, blog-series-structure.md, content-calendar-final.md
- **Dependencies:** Phase 4 ✅

### Phase 6: Organization & Tracking (Week 3, Days 3-5)
**File:** `PHASE-6-ORGANIZATION-TRACKING.md`
- **Goal:** Build master tracking system + reporting
- **Time:** 4-5 hours
- **Key Tasks:**
  - Master keyword tracker (120+ keywords in one CSV)
  - Google Search Console setup (position tracking)
  - Google Analytics 4 setup (conversion goals)
  - Monthly reporting template
  - Baseline metrics capture
  - Publishing workflow checklist
- **Outputs:** SEO-MASTER-TRACKER.csv, MONTHLY-REPORT-TEMPLATE.md, CONTENT-PUBLISHING-WORKFLOW.md
- **Dependencies:** Phase 5 ✅

### Phase 7: Tools & Automation (Week 4, Days 1-3)
**File:** `PHASE-7-TOOLS-AUTOMATION.md`
- **Goal:** Set up free tools + automation workflows
- **Time:** 4-5 hours
- **Key Tasks:**
  - Free tools stack setup (GSC, GA4, Planner)
  - Rank tracking setup (manual or automated)
  - Google Sheets automation (monthly reports)
  - Content workflow tools (Trello/Airtable/GitHub)
  - Email alerts + backlink monitoring
- **Outputs:** TOOLS-STACK-SUMMARY.md, RANK-TRACKING-SETUP.md, GOOGLE-SHEETS-SETUP.md
- **Dependencies:** Phase 6 ✅

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Start with Phase 1:** Don't skip keyword research, even if you're eager to write
2. **Use spreadsheets:** Keep everything in CSVs for easy collaboration
3. **Review Phase 4 weekly:** Competitors' content changes, update your analysis
4. **Publish consistently:** 1-2 posts per week for 12 weeks > 24 posts at once
5. **Track everything:** Use SEO-MASTER-TRACKER.csv as your source of truth
6. **Build internal links:** Every blog post links to 3-5 others (topic cluster)
7. **Update and refresh:** Old content with new data ranks better
8. **Monitor keywords:** Check Google Search Console weekly (quick wins appear fast!)
