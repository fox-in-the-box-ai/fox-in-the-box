### Phase 5: Opportunity Scoring

**Manual Scoring Rubric:**
```
Opportunity Score = (Search Volume - Difficulty) × Relevance
                   + Your Unique Advantage

- Search Volume: 1-10 (estimated)
- Difficulty: 1-10 (estimated from SERP authority)
```
